1.0 ANOMALY DESCRIPTION

1.1 Physical Description

ARC-303 is a temporal predator with no physically stable form. It manifests as a momentary perceptual distortion or a moving shadow. On rare occasions, its silhouette, visible in reflections, has a humanoid structure but with unnaturally long limbs. Where its face should be, there is a smooth, dark void.

1.2 Anomalous Effect and Behavior

It "eats" personal timelines—memories, existence, and traces in history. This occurs in three stages: Stalking, The Feed, and The Hollow Echo, after which only a biological shell of the victim remains.
