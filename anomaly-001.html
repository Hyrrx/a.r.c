File: Anomaly ARC-001

Object Class: Safe

Containment Procedures

Object is to be kept in a standard secure locker at Site-19. Access does not require special clearance.

Description

ARC-001 is a common fountain pen of indeterminate brand, discovered in the year ████. When used to write, it does not require ink. Any text written with the pen will slowly fade and disappear completely within 24 hours, leaving no trace on the paper.

Analysis of the pen's material has been inconclusive, though it shows properties similar to those found at the ████████████ incident site. Its anomalous effects cannot be disabled.
